Privacy Policy for Hoshikuzu

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hoshikuzu ("we", "our", or "the Game"), a Telegram Mini App, handles data. Hoshikuzu is built as a client-side serverless application. We value your privacy and do not collect personal data on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Because Hoshikuzu runs directly within the Telegram Webview environment, we interact with minimal data to ensure your game progress functions correctly:

  • Telegram User ID & Authentication Data: Provided securely via the Telegram Mini Apps API to identify your account session.
  • Game Progress State: Information about your game state, including your level, Coins, Exp, spins, collected anime cards, and purchased game upgrades.

2. How We Store Your Data

Hoshikuzu uses a client-side architecture. Your game progress is stored locally on your device and within the Telegram official infrastructure:

  • Browser LocalStorage: Used to temporarily save your data directly inside your local Telegram Webview cache.
  • Telegram CloudStorage: Used to securely synchronize your game saves across different devices utilizing official Telegram developer APIs.

We do not own, maintain, or operate any independent database servers to collect, harvest, or store your personal identity information.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game integrates third-party services for monetization and ad delivery. These providers may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • AdsGram: An advertising network utilized to deliver rewarded video ads and banners within the game environment. AdsGram may process identifiers to display ads and track view completions.
  • Telegram Stars Platform: All financial transactions for in-game premium upgrades are securely processed through the Telegram ecosystem using Telegram Stars. We do not collect or store your payment details, credit card numbers, or billing data.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since your data is entirely hosted within your device's browser cache (LocalStorage) and Telegram CloudStorage, you have full control over it. Clearing your Telegram local application data or cache may reset your local progress. You can request data clearance inside the Telegram cloud systems using native platform settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Hoshikuzu does not knowingly collect data from children. The game content is intended for general audiences compliant with Telegram's core Terms of Service policies regarding age limits restrictions.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer directly inside the Telegram platform through the official Hoshikuzu game channel or support bot link.
